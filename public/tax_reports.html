Tax reports

for 2017 fiscal year

The tax reports are based on the application of the current legal framework available for the taxation of digital currencies in the investorʼs jurisdiction. The methodology used for processing of the reports is approved by a Big 4 Accounting Firm. Reports will be prepared by our partner CryptoTax. The tax reports do not constitute tax advice. Please consult your own tax advisor with any questions.

What report do you need?

Switzerland report

Deadline: 31 Jan 2018
  1. Customer letter.
  2. Application of the big four certified Swiss tax logic (including Income and Wealth Tax) and illustration of the results.
  3. Form assistant document for the “Wertschriften-und Guthabenverzeichnis” for the Swiss tax declaration.
  4. Explanation document regarding the form assistant document(s) and calculations.

Generic report

Deadline: 31 Jan 2018
  1. Customer letter.
  2. Summary of the income amount.
  3. Report including all transactions with the corresponding income calculation according to the FIFO method.
  4. Explanation document regarding the evaluation and income calculation of all transactions.

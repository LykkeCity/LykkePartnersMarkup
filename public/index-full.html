KYC\AML checks for partners’ users
Email Verification

Email Verification

According to Anti Money Laundering directive Lykke has to perform KYC checks for all users trading at Lykke Exchange, including the partners users.

KYC requirements differ from the different regions and a type of license used to onboard clients. But in most cases the following documents are required:

  • Selfie
    Not required under UK AML law but let’s keep it for now as you state “in most cases”
  • ID or Passport or Driving license
  • Proof of address

Currently Lykke onboards clients under UK regulations that require a user to submit the documents listed above. Once Lykke obtains an OTF license in Switzerland, the onboarding procedure for Switzerland citizens will include a video validation according to FINMA rules.

All the documents and information such as name, phone number, etc. received from users are stored at Lykke’s servers in Switzerland and available to Lykke KYC officers in Lykke backoffice.

Lykke KYC officers perform KYC checks for all partners’ users, a partner pays fee for KYC check per user to Lykke (Please see Cost of services).

Scenarios

The following scenarios are possible:

  • A user already signed up to LykkeWallet and passed KYC check. The users signs up to a partner’s application. If a partner doesn’t have additional requirements regarding the KYC procedure, there is no need for the user to pass KYC check in the partner’s application. The partner doesn’t pay fees for the KYC check.
  • A user has signed up to LykkeWallet and while the KYC check on him/her is being processed the user signs up to a partner's application. The user needs to submit all the required KYC documents in the partner’s application. The partner pays fees.
  • A user signed up to Lykke Wallet and passed KYC check. According to KYC\AML rules after some period of time the user needs to re-submit the documents. The user has re-submitted the required documents and while the KYC check on him/her is being processed the user signs up to a partner's application. The user needs to submit all the required KYC documents in the partner’s application. The partner pays fees.

Note

Lykke KYC officers cannot change the order of users for performing KYC checks and don’t have an ability to visually recognize partners’ users from Lykke Wallet users.

Partner’s Terms of Use shall include the following statement “KYC is processed by Lykke and by accepting this clients agree to share with their data with Lykke”.

How to start using LykkeWallet?

  1. Download your free Lykke Wallet app for iOS or Android.
  2. Register your Lykke Wallet.
  3. Provide a photo, ID and proof of address. The app guides you through the process. Five minutes, and you’re done.
